Our Mission

Our set out mission and the ways and manners we set out to achieve it are subject to regular reviews and we accept advice.

Our mission is to provide to our pupils and students with that initial right education, so that they may lead a fulfilled lives.

The school will strives to present the highest quality of teaching and learning. 

Each child is encouraged and stimulated to aim for excellence and achieve the best possible personal and academic standards.

We valued diversity and richness of contributions from teachers in upholding and promoting all round high standards. 

Emphasis is placed in self discipline, the right use of freedom and graceful personal achievement growth. Teachers, Pupils/Students and Staff are expected to treat each others with respect and responsibility.

Welcome To Evergreen Schools Sokoto
Like an Evergreen ( 🌲 ) Tree, we believe in a continuous cycle of renewal of an excellent outstanding education in a caring and inclusive environment, for our Pupils and Students ages 3 up to 16/17 years.

  • We aim to nurture and inspire, bringing out Pupils and Students interest throughout their entire School life, so they become confident, enthusiastic and well-rounded people, both now and in the future.

    Our excellent grades reflect the outstanding teaching delivered at Evergreen Schools, we offer standard rounded programme of activities to bring out the talents of every child in a joyful and happy manner.

    We have the unshakeable belief that a young person who's happy is much more likely to be an effective learner.

    Take a look around to get a test of what we have to offer, I would be delighted to meet you.
